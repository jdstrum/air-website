The Imagination Gap

Every transformation starts with imagination.

AI is fundamentally changing the way we work. Think bigger and the possibilities become endless.

Most companies are using AI to do the same work slightly faster. The ones pulling ahead are reimagining the work entirely.

Find your industry below for use cases that are transforming the way businesses operate. Each one maps to a readiness dimension.

Healthcare

AI in healthcare goes far beyond clinical decision support. The biggest opportunities are in the operational layers most systems haven't touched.

Customer Experience

AI triage that routes patients before they hit the waiting room

Symptom-aware intake systems assess urgency, pre-populate charts, and route patients to the right department — cutting average wait-to-treatment time significantly.

30–45% reduction in ER wait-to-treatment time
Data Readiness

Automated prior authorization that resolves in minutes, not days

AI cross-references patient records, payer requirements, and clinical guidelines to auto-generate auth requests — and auto-approves the ones that clearly qualify.

85% of authorizations resolved same-day
Workforce

Ambient clinical documentation that gives physicians back 2 hours a day

AI listens to patient-provider conversations and generates structured clinical notes in real time. Physicians review and sign instead of typing from memory at midnight.

2+ hours returned to patient care per clinician per day
Governance

Real-time compliance monitoring across every AI-generated document

Every AI output — discharge summaries, referral letters, insurance communications — is automatically scanned for HIPAA violations, coding errors, and regulatory gaps before it leaves the system.

60%+ fewer compliance errors before documents leave the system
Strategy

Predictive readmission scoring that triggers intervention before discharge

AI identifies high-risk patients 48 hours before discharge and auto-generates care coordination plans — social work referrals, follow-up scheduling, medication reconciliation — while they're still in-house.

23% reduction in 30-day readmissions
Technology

Cross-system patient data unification without ripping out your EHR

AI middleware creates a unified patient view across Epic, Cerner, billing, pharmacy, and lab systems — surfacing conflicts and gaps that no single system can see.

Single patient view across 5+ systems in weeks, not years
Customer Experience

Post-discharge follow-up that actually happens

AI-powered outreach contacts every discharged patient with personalized check-ins, medication reminders, and escalation triggers — no staff time required for the 80% who are recovering normally.

3x increase in post-discharge engagement

Financial Services

Financial services firms are sitting on more data than almost any other industry. The ones pulling ahead are using AI to make that data work across silos.

Data Readiness

Real-time fraud detection that learns your customers' patterns

AI models trained on your specific transaction data catch anomalies that generic rule-based systems miss — and reduce false positives that frustrate legitimate customers.

60% fewer false positives, 40% more true fraud caught
Workforce

Relationship managers who walk in knowing everything

AI assembles a real-time client brief before every meeting — recent transactions, life events, product gaps, competitor moves, and next-best-action recommendations.

28% increase in cross-sell conversion
Governance

Automated regulatory reporting that writes itself

AI pulls data from trading systems, compliance logs, and risk databases to auto-generate SEC, FINRA, and Basel III reports — with audit trails that regulators actually trust.

70% reduction in report preparation time
Customer Experience

Claims processing that closes in hours, not weeks

AI reads claim documents, cross-references policy terms, pulls comparable historical claims, and generates a recommended payout — routing only the edge cases to human adjusters.

80% of straightforward claims auto-resolved
Strategy

Portfolio risk modeling that sees around corners

AI continuously scans market data, geopolitical signals, supply chain disruptions, and client concentration risk to surface vulnerabilities before they become losses.

Early warning signals 2–3 weeks ahead of traditional models
Technology

Legacy system modernization without the five-year rewrite

AI translates COBOL and mainframe logic into modern APIs, creating a bridge layer that lets new AI applications talk to 30-year-old core banking systems.

80% faster integration vs. full system replacement
Data Readiness

KYC and AML screening that actually scales

AI processes new client onboarding documents, screens against sanctions lists, and flags discrepancies — turning a 3-week process into a 3-day one without cutting corners on compliance.

5x faster client onboarding

Manufacturing has more sensor data than it knows what to do with. AI turns that data from noise into operational advantage.

Technology

Predictive maintenance that schedules itself

AI analyzes vibration, temperature, and performance data from equipment sensors to predict failures 2–4 weeks out — and auto-schedules maintenance during planned downtime windows.

45% reduction in unplanned downtime
Data Readiness

Quality control that catches defects humans can't see

Computer vision systems inspect products at line speed, catching micro-defects, dimensional drift, and surface anomalies that escape manual inspection — and learning from every miss.

90%+ defect detection rate at full line speed
Strategy

Demand forecasting that accounts for signals your ERP ignores

AI combines sales data with weather patterns, social media trends, competitor pricing, and supply chain signals to predict demand with far more accuracy than historical averages.

35% improvement in forecast accuracy
Workforce

Digital work instructions that adapt to the operator

AI-powered AR overlays guide operators through complex assembly with step-by-step visual instructions — adjusting complexity based on experience level and error history.

60% faster new-hire proficiency
Governance

Supply chain provenance tracking from raw material to finished good

AI creates an auditable chain of custody for every component — flagging conflict minerals, tracking carbon footprint, and generating ESG compliance reports automatically.

End-to-end provenance visibility for compliance and ESG reporting
Customer Experience

Custom configuration quoting in minutes instead of days

AI translates customer specs into manufacturing BOMs, checks feasibility against current capacity, and generates accurate quotes — including lead times — while the sales call is still happening.

Quote turnaround from 5 days to 15 minutes
Technology

Energy optimization that cuts utility costs without cutting output

AI dynamically adjusts HVAC, compressed air, lighting, and equipment scheduling based on production schedules, energy prices, and weather forecasts.

15–25% reduction in energy costs

25% improvement in retention for flagged students

12% improvement in enrollment yield at same aid budget

Data-driven program decisions instead of committee politics
Governance

AI automatically collects, tags, and organizes the evidence needed for SACSCOC, HLC, or programmatic accreditation — so the report writes itself by the time the visit arrives.

Accreditation prep from 18 months to 3 months
Technology

Intelligent scheduling that eliminates room and faculty conflicts

AI optimizes course scheduling across room capacity, faculty preferences, student demand patterns, and prerequisite chains — producing conflict-free schedules that maximize utilization.

95% conflict-free schedules, 20% better room utilization
Customer Experience

Personalized learning pathways that adapt in real time

AI adjusts content difficulty, format, and pacing based on how each student is performing — surfacing additional practice where they struggle and accelerating where they don't.

Measurable improvement in course completion rates
