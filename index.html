Six dimensions of AI readiness.

We measure six things. Each one tells you something different about where you are and what comes next.

01

Strategic Vision & Leadership

Does your leadership have a plan for this, or are they just reacting to pressure? Strategy determines whether the investment pays off.

02

Data Readiness

Your data is spread across dozens of systems that were never built to talk to each other. Everything downstream depends on fixing that.

03

Technology Infrastructure

Can your network and compute environment actually handle these workloads? The gap is physical and measurable.

04

Governance & Security

Who's using what, what data is going where, and what rules exist to manage it. Without guardrails, nothing else holds.

05

Experience Layer

Phone, chat, email, video, collaboration — every channel your customers and teams touch. This is where the ROI shows up first.

06

Workforce & Adoption

Your people aren't waiting. They're already building with these tools on their own. The real question is whether the organization is keeping up.
