Last updated: March 14, 2026

AI Resulting ("we," "us," or "our") operates the airesulting.com website and the AI Readiness Assessment platform. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

What We Collect

When you take the AI Readiness Assessment, we collect: your name, email address, company name, job title, company size range, and your responses to the assessment questions. If you opt in to speak with a Resultant, we share your assessment results and contact information with the assigned advisor.

How We Use Your Information

Your assessment responses are used to generate your personalized AI Readiness Report. That is the primary and intended use. We may also use aggregated, anonymized data to improve the assessment framework and publish general industry insights. We will never identify you or your company in any published material without your explicit permission.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your individual responses with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to train public AI models. Your assessment responses are not used for advertising or retargeting.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Assessment data is retained for the purpose of generating and delivering your report and for facilitating any Resultant engagement you opt into. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the assessment to function (session management). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Third-Party Services

AI Resulting is a partner of Bridgepointe Technologies. If you opt in to a Resultant engagement, your assessment data will be shared with your assigned Resultant within the Bridgepointe Technologies network. No data is shared until you explicitly opt in.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
