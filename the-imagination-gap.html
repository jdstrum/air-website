Real Estate

Agentic AI is reshaping how properties are managed, leased, and valued — turning a dozen manual handoffs into orchestrated workflows.

Technology

Maintenance orchestration that resolves issues before tenants wake up

A sensor flags a leak at 6 a.m. An AI agent identifies the unit, alerts maintenance, grants smart-lock access, dispatches a vendor, and drafts resident notices with arrival windows — replacing a dozen manual calls with an automated chain.

30%+ time savings on maintenance workflows
Customer Experience

Lead-to-lease automation that catches churn before it starts

AI handles the full journey from lead to signed lease — timely communication, document capture, follow-through. Then it monitors for churn signals: repeated unresolved issues, complaint patterns, slower response behavior. Intervention happens before the renewal window closes.

Higher conversion rates, measurably lower tenant churn
Data Readiness

Investment analysis that assembles itself from live portfolio data

AI agents search structured lease and operating data, draft investment committee materials with clear sourcing, detect early warning signals across the portfolio, and auto-update when numbers change — speed, consistency, and auditability in every decision.

$430–550B in annual value potential across real estate globally
